# Validation Rules

- `email` must be unique and valid format
- `password` must be at least 8 characters
- `bus_number` must be unique and non-empty
- `route.name` must be non-empty
- `stop.name` must be non-empty
- `latitude` must be between `-90` and `90`
- `longitude` must be between `-180` and `180`
- `stop_order` must be zero or greater
- `estimated_minutes_from_start` must be zero or greater
- `speed` must be zero or greater
